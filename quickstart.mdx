## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: GitHub or Google account for social login

<Callout kind="info">

Prepare your initial documentation files in Markdown or MDX format. Recommended tools include VS Code with MDX extensions for local editing.

</Callout>

## Sign Up for an Account

Create your account to access the full features of Сергій Москалюк documentation platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://app.example.com`.

    Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user-plus">
    Fill in your email address, create a strong password, and provide your full name.

    Agree to the terms and click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from `no-reply@example.com`.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In Using Your Preferred Method

Choose from multiple login options for quick access.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email & Password" icon="mail">
    Enter your registered email and password on the login page.

    Click **Log In**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google" icon="google">
    Click the **Continue with Google** button.

    Select your Google account and authorize the app.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GitHub" icon="github">
    Click **Continue with GitHub**.

    Approve the OAuth request to link your repository access.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in your account settings after first login for added security.

</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Project

Set up a new space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="folder-plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Documentation Space**.

    Enter a name like `my-project-docs` and select a template (e.g., "Blank" or "API Docs").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Basics" icon="settings">
    Add a description: "Documentation for My Awesome Project".

    Set the brand color to `#3B82F6` for your theme.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Publish" icon="save">
    Click **Create Space**. Your project is now live at `https://docs.example.com/my-project-docs`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Your Initial Content

Add your first pages using these methods.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Drag & Drop Files" icon="upload" horizontal>
    Navigate to the **Content** section.

    Drag Markdown or MDX files directly into the upload area.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Inline Editor" icon="edit-3">
    Click **New Page** and use the built-in MDX editor.

    Paste or write your content, then **Publish**.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Here's an example of your first MDX page frontmatter:

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
  ```mdx
---
title: Introduction
description: Welcome to my project docs.
---

## Overview

Get started here.
```
  ```markdown
---
title: Introduction
description: Welcome to my project docs.
---

# Overview <!-- Note: Use H1 only in raw Markdown -->

Get started here.
```
</CodeGroup>

## Navigate and Edit Your Space

Master basic navigation to manage your docs efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Explore Sidebar" icon="menu">
    Use the left sidebar for pages, settings, and previews.

    Click page names to edit or view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit a Page" icon="edit">
    Select a page, make changes in the editor.

    Preview with **Live Preview** and **Publish** updates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Theme" icon="palette">
    Go to **Settings {gt} Theme**.

    Update brand color, logo, and navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
