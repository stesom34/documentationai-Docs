---
title: Cannot Access Documentation
description: Quick resolution steps if you cannot access your docs.
---

## Problem

- Page says "Not Authorized" or "404 Not Found"
- Project docs won't load

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login status">
    ````
    Make sure you are logged in to your Сергій Москалюк account.
    Logout & log in again if unsure.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    ````
    Ensure your account has access to the specific project documentation.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    ````
    Try refreshing or clearing browser cache and cookies.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
